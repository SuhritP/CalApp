Privacy Policy

Image to Google Calendar AI-Powered App

Last updated: January 6, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Image to Google Calendar ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application that converts class schedule images to Google Calendar events using AI technology.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Images You Upload

We temporarily process images of class schedules or calendars that you voluntarily upload to extract event information using AI technology. These images are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.

2.2 Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information including your name, email address, and profile picture. This information is used solely for authentication purposes.

2.3 Google Calendar Access

We request permission to add events to your Google Calendar. We only create new events based on the information you choose to add and do not read, modify, or delete existing calendar events.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process uploaded images to extract event information using AI
  • Authenticate your identity through Google OAuth
  • Create calendar events in your Google Calendar as requested
  • Provide and improve our service functionality
  • Ensure the security and proper operation of our application

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 No Data Storage

We do not store your uploaded images, extracted text, or calendar event data on our servers. All processing is done in real-time, and data is discarded immediately after processing.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and secure API key management for third-party services.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Services

Our application integrates with Google Calendar API and Google OAuth. Your use of Google services is also governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API to process images and extract event information. Image data sent to OpenAI is processed according to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in this privacy policy. We may share information only when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can revoke Google Calendar access at any time through your Google Account settings
  • You control what images you upload and what events you choose to add to your calendar
  • You can stop using our service at any time
  • You can request information about your data by contacting us

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@imagetocalendar.app

Website: Image to Google Calendar

11. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing of your personal data. Since we do not store your data, most of these rights are automatically fulfilled by our data handling practices.

12. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not sell personal information and do not store user data, many CCPA protections are inherently provided by our service design.
